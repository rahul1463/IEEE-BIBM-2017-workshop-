IEEE BIBM 2017 Workshop

ABOUT Workshop

Study of the deformation / degeneracy and DNA RNA protein structure of biomedical images /microscopic cell images using computational methods

Study of biomedical images in macro level and microscopic cell images in micro level under any change in intra or extracellular condition is the objective or interest of pre-sent proposal. The proposal contains an extensive study of any deformation / diseases which may be captured both at anatomical level through the medical imaging process and also at the cellular level using microscopic techniques. This may further lead to the background research in this direction even at the DNA/ RNA and protein structure level indicating the nature of cell growth, its mortality, its proliferation and any other changes or deformation due to diseases like cancer and other. Objective of this proposal will contain the subsequent researches which will reflect the study of deformation using the image analysis through computational process both at macro and micro levels.
